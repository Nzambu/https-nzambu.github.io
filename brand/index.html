Corporate Identity

This is external “personality” projected by a corporation, Logo is often the center of company branding, since it is an easily recognizable symbol which sets the corporation aside from other companies. This is the personality that consistently runs through your business cards, Letterheads and all your office stationery.

Marketing Materials

This can be a virtual one, in PDF/JPEG format on the internet or a handout with basic information about your company. i.e. Flyers, Brochures, Company profiles, Documents Folders, Newsletters e.t.c

Outdoor Adverstising

This is one of the oldest and most enduring forms of advertising; any advertising done outdoors that publicizes your business’s products and services. Such as signage, Roll up banners, Street banners, Snapper frames, Billboards, window graphics e.t.c

Branded Apparel

This include Clothing, especially outer garments that publicize your company using your Logo i.e. T-shirts, Poloshirts, Dustcoats, Overalls, Hoodies, Sweaters, scarves, headwear, caps e.t.c.

Promotional Items

This can include sales collateral material, advertisements and even small, everyday products that contain the company logo. Promotional products can serve many purposes before or after a sale. i.e. Branded uniforms, bags, Mugs, pens, Diaries, Coasters, Mouse Pads, Desk Clocks, e.t.c
